FAQs

Do I need to be home?

No—many clients provide keyed access or codes. We can clean during work hours or while you’re away.

Do you bring supplies?

Yes, we bring standard materials and equipment. If you prefer specific brands, we can use yours.

Can I change my schedule?

Absolutely. Let us know 24 hours in advance to reschedule. Recurring clients can adjust any time.

Are you insured?

Yes, we’re fully insured for domestic and commercial work for peace of mind.

What areas do you cover?

We focus on the local area. Tell us your postcode on the quote form and we’ll confirm coverage.

What about pets?

Pet-friendly! Please mention any pets or special instructions—doors, feeding times, or nervous animals.
